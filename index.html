Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Kinder education focuses on playful learning, creativity, and basic skills in language and math, helping children grow socially and emotionally.

Elementary School

Elementary education builds foundational skills in core subjects, fostering curiosity, teamwork, and problem-solving.

Middle (10-16) Years

Middle school enhances critical thinking, independence, and confidence, preparing students for future challenges.
